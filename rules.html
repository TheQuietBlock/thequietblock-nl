Server Rules

Simple, stable rules to protect progression and keep the world fair.

Server: mc.thequietblock.nl

Full Rule Set

  1. Respect other players.

    No harassment, no toxic behavior, no real-world drama spillover. Keep chat civil. This is a shared world, not a battleground of egos.

  2. No griefing or stealing.

    Do not break, modify, or take anything that isn’t yours without permission. Even “small stuff” counts. Trust is the foundation of survival multiplayer.

  3. PvP by agreement only.

    Combat is allowed only if both players agree beforehand. No trap bases, no lava ambushes, no surprise kill farms targeting players.

  4. No cheats, exploits, or unfair mods.

    No hacked clients, x-ray, duplication glitches, auto-clickers, or abuse of game-breaking bugs. Performance mods like Sodium or optimization tools are fine as long as they don’t give gameplay advantages.

  5. Build responsibly.

    No lag machines, excessive entity farms, or redstone clocks that constantly run. If your build tanks TPS, you’ll be asked to optimize it. Survival is hard enough without fighting the server.

  6. Respect world integrity.

    No random destruction of terrain just because “it’s survival.” Don’t leave floating trees, giant creeper craters, or strip-mined wastelands near spawn. Clean up after yourself.

  7. Claim and space your base.

    Don’t build too close to others without asking. Mark your base clearly with signs. Spawn area stays clean and welcoming.

  8. AFK farming limits.

    Short AFK sessions are fine. 24/7 AFK setups that monopolize mob caps or stress the server are not allowed.

  9. Admin decisions are final.

    Admins maintain server stability and fairness. Backups exist, but they are for accidents, not arguments.

  10. Play survival as intended.

    No creative access, no spawned items, no special privileges unless explicitly stated. Everyone earns their progress.

That’s it. Simple. Stable. Sustainable.

The Quiet Block is an independent community and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or associated with Mojang Studios or Microsoft.
